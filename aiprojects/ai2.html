Shakespeare's Poetry Creator

Prithvi Raj TiwariDec 1, 2020

This project writes poetry in the Shakespeare’s Style. The model is trained on the large number of shakepeare’s poems. Just give it some starting text and the model will continue it in form of poem upto 100 words.



Technologies Used:-
  • 1.TensorFlow
  • 2.Python
  • 3.Recurrent Neural Nets
  • 4.Deep Learning(AI)
